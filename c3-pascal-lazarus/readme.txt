C3Lang's Lazarus version, because I have too much legacy Delphi code, C3Lang directly supports (optional) Pascal code, but it is a subset with certain constraints.

pascal 的解析部分会放在独立的文件中，不喜欢这些老语法的网友直接去掉它们就可以了。

The parsing part of Pascal will be placed in a separate file, and netizens who do not like these old syntax can simply remove them.
